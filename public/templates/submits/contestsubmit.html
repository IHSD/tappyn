What is a CTR %?

CTR stands for Click Through Rate and is the primary metric for the effectiveness of an ad. As you create ads on Tappyn, they are automatically shown equally to a company's specific audience for a short amount of time. As more people click on your ad, that ad's CTR increases. The better your ad is, the higher CTR your ad will recieve, and it's more likely that a company will Tapp your ad!
