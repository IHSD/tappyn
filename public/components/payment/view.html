Payment FAQ

Stripe, a globally trusted payment processor, requires the card information to set up a direct deposit system with the associated account of your choosing. We do not store this account information, and your card will never be charged under any circumstances. If you would like to remove a payment method, please contact us through our chat or at squad@tappyn.com, and we'll take care of it immediately. If you are an international user or you'd prefer not to be paid through Stripe, we also offer payouts via PayPal. Simply contact us through our chat or at squad@tappyn.com with your PayPal address after you've won a contest, and we'll promptly transfer your winnings. Stripe payouts are 100% safe. Tappyn does not store your card details or any financial information, and all transactions go only through Stripe. Feel free to contact us if you have any additional concerns!
