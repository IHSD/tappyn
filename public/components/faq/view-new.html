About Fabel

Fabel is a place to create authentic ads for people like you. Businesses looking for ads will launch a campaign, and you'll have the opportunity to create an ad for them based on your personal experiences. Most ads only ask for a sentence or two, but some ask you to select a photo you think would perform well. Ads on Fabel only require a few minutes to make, and you'll earn $20-$50 for every ad that a company purchases. Campaigns are hosted by cool, new companies willing to pay an immediate $20-$50 for digital ads created by you. They are capped at 30 ad submissions and end after 7 days or if they reach their cap limit first. At the close of the campaign, the hosting company chooses the ads that they love, and the creators of those ads are instantly paid. Other members of Fabel can up-vote your copy, bringing it to the top of the campaign feed for the company to see. Submit to as many campaigns as you'd like! When an ad gets purchased, the creator of that ad gets immediately paid!

Dashboard

You can see the campaign status that your ad was submitted to right in your dashboard. Here's a breakdown of what they mean...
-Live: This means that a campaign hasn't ended yet, and more ads are in the process of being created. Once a campaign reaches its cap of 30 ads or time runs out, it will be ready to start testing.
-Pending Testing: The campaign has ended and we're about to start testing your ads in real time on a live platform.
-Testing: Selected ads are tested for approximately 24 hours so both you and the company hosting the campaign can see how they perform.
-Pending Selection: Testing has been completed, and you should be able to see the CTR (Click Through Rate) that your ad generated in your dashboard.
-Complete: This final status means that the campaign has ended, testing is done, and the company that hosted the campaign has purchased their favorite ads.

Choosing A Winner

From your dashboard, click on the campaign that has recently closed and press "Choose Winner" underneath your favorite ad. This is where you and the writer sign the Ad Transfer Agreement, which is automatically executed when you choose a winning ad. After the transaction has occured,you own all rights to that advertisement. You have 3 days from when the campaign closes to choose a winner. Otherwise, we'll pick the one with the highest up-votes to complete your campaign.
